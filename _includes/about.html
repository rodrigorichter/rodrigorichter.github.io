About Me

When I got my first computer at age 6(with an incredible 2GB hard drive), I played with it all the time. Since I was 12, I've worked on games. I ran servers, made 3d maps, 3d models, designed and coded games, made art with Photoshop, produced videos and websites. I remember the long hours trying to fix bugs in programs and systems I barely knew anything about. It was chaos, but I had so much fun! I graduated at UFRGS, where I worked in a data visualization research project. In my free time, I develop games, read books and bike around.
